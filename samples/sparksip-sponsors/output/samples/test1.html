(3T) Tiny Tidy Task Bots

 
Description
You are invited to explore, design, and create tiny task robots. You will research about existing mechanical and electrical systems that underlie common daily things like vacuum cleaners. And you will move to design and create your own systems for cleaning small areas. You will create programmable mechanical systems that dramatically change how we clean our spaces. As a stretch exercise you might create a system of interconnected bots to work together in harmony.

For this internship, a team of 2-3 students are welcome too. Please apply as a team.


The participants will research about following:


a) System Analysis - understand deeply about how commonly daily thinks work. In particular you will start with the vacuum cleaners
b) System Design and Prototyping - design and prototype with paper models and move towards assembling small systems
c) Programming - program small computer systems to operate in command and control mode

Requirements


The following qualifications are required:
1. Tinkering Skills - you can do hands-dirty exploration of everyday objects. Tear them apart and put them back together in working conditions.
2. Passion for Physics, Math, Mechanical, and Electrical things
3. Research and survey of existing systems.
3. Programming Skills (preferred if you had played with robotics and small systems)
